My Academic Background

Learning is like breathing for me.

Meram Science High School

This is my high school. I was sitting at the very back of the classroom and not listening the literature and geography lectures in those years. My favorite class was physics and I had the highest grades in that. I mostly enjoyed playing basketball with my buddies.

Bogazici University

I can identify there as the place of my second birth because I was mentally born there. I started studying Electrical&Electronics Engineering in 2017. I worked as an undergrad research assistant at BOUNTENNA(Antennas and Propagation Research Laboratory) in my first year. Third year, I went to an exchange semester to Technical University of Denmark. Due to my interest in physics, I took 6 extra physics classes as my elective courses from physics department. I also chose Communication Systems specialization option in my last year. I worked as a undergrad research assistant for the microprocessors course. My bachelor project title was "Anomaly Detection in Large Hadron Collider Data using Autoencoders". I graduated from there in June 2023 with a cumulative GPA of 3.5/4.00. Furthermore, my favorite elective classes were tennis, music and visual arts, yoga and introduction to psychology. Taking many classes from different areas helps me to look at things from a wider perspective.
